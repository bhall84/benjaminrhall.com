The Gap

The Gap Between AI Policy and AI Reality Is a Training Problem

Most organizations are navigating AI adoption with no internal training capacity, no budget structured for it, and no vendor relationship that prioritizes how adults actually learn.

A board member champions a new tool. A district issues a policy. A funder asks what your AI strategy is. The people doing the work are left to figure it out on their own.
